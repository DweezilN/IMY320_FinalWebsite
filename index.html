The Chronicles of Arrg

The Chronicles of arrg is a is a game where the player needs to help Gibbs navigate and fight his way through treacherous terrains and enemies, back to his ship which has been overrun by Skeleton Pirates.
This 2D platformer game makes use of a grappling hook which is not only used to move around, but also to dodge barrels, bottles of poison and even enemies. Close combat is inevitable, but do not fret, Gibbs is no stranger to his sword.

Can you help Gibbs get back to his ship?

Level 1 After drinking too much, Gibbs wakes up in the local tavern, only to discover that the Skeletons have overrun his ship. Gibbs must traverse through the tavern and defeat all of the enemies who stayed behind to keep him there.

Level 2 Outside of the tavern, Gibbs has to pass through the skeleton infested caves in order to get to his ship before those pesky pirates destroy it. Whatever you do, don’t fall on the rocks, they are quite sharp.

Level 3 Finally back on his ship, Gibbs notices that the skeleton pirates have run his ship into some rocks! Gibbs has to fight his way through the ship to get above deck, where he will have to face his biggest enemy and leader of the skeletons.
